Education in Engineering

Did you know that Engineering is the second most popular major taken in universities? According to a study by Top Universities, Engineering and Technology ranks second in 10 most popular fields of study, dominating major fields like health care, business and law. The best university for Engineering is also considered as Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). The field of engineering has five major subfields of which include Civil Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Mechanical engineering, Electrical Engineering and Industrial Engineering.
