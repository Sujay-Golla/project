Civil Engineering

What is Civil Engineering?

Civil Engineering is often thought as the oldest engineering discipline, which mainly focuses on designing, constructing and maintaining physical structures that are used by the public, which includes dams, bridges, tunnels, roads, airports, subway systems, and water supply systems. Today’s engineers specialize in building eco-friendly structures, to help combat climate change.
